- Account, customer, subscription, contract, and invoice management
- Usage metering and rated billing
- Tax calculation and jurisdiction handling
- Payments, refunds, credits, and dunning
- Revenue recognition and general ledger export
- Auditability, idempotency, and regulatory controls
